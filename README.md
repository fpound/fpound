## Hi, I'm Forrest 👋

I'm a geospatial data scientist and data engineer building production platforms and novel risk models. My work sits at the seam between climate/environmental risk and the data infrastructure that makes hard problems tractable at scale.

Most of my current work lives in private client repos. A few things I can point to:

### Featured Work

- **[SPOT-AIS](https://spot-ais.org)** — Web-based mapping platform I designed and lead-engineered at Motivf for ~100 users across the U.S. Army Corps of Engineers and state aquatic invasive species programs. Built on a Databricks pipeline processing hundreds of billions of GPS trajectory points.

- **Resonance** — Human mobility analytics platform I architect at Motivf, serving federal and commercial clients. Case study at [forestpound.com](https://forestpound.com).

- **Wildfire structural vulnerability research** — Neighborhood-scale survivability modeling at WUUII using NDVI-derived vegetation features and random forest classification. Case study at [forestpound.com](https://forestpound.com).

### Background

Before geospatial, nearly two decades of documentary filmmaking for clients including High Country News, The Long Now Foundation, and The Redford Center. The throughline is making complex technical work legible to people who need to act on it.
